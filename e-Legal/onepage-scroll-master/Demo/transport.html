Road and Transport

Our Vision

"To provide and facilitate transport- related services to the people with a thrust on speed, safety, environment-friendliness and fuel-efficiency in a transparent and citizen-friendly fashion, thus accelerating the growth and development and thereby furthering the interests of the State and Nation."

Our Mission

Providing transport related services easily accessible to common man and ensuring Road Safety.

  • Issuance of computerized Learner's Licence in the State.
  • Issuance of 4 KB SCOSTA based Smart-Card Driving Licence in the State.
  • Issuance of Optical-Card Vehicle Registration Book (VRC).
  • Issuance of Computerized Permits.
  • Issuance of computerized Fitness Certificate of vehicles.
  • Introduction of e-Payment System.
  • Creation of State Register.
  • Issuance and termination of Hypothecation entry of vehicles in computerized form.
  • Introduction of Web-based solution and SMS gateways.
  • Modernization of Check-posts.
